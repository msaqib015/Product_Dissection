# 🍽️ Product Dissection for Zomato: Food Delivery and Dining

This project provides a detailed product analysis of **Zomato**, focusing on its **Food Delivery** and **Dining Out** verticals. The goal is to break down Zomato’s product from a product management perspective — evaluating its features, user experience, monetization, and market positioning.

---

## 🧠 Objective

To dissect Zomato’s product experience and ecosystem using industry-standard frameworks and tools, with the aim of understanding:
- Key features and value propositions
- User flows and UX/UI elements
- Growth, retention, and monetization strategies
- Competitive positioning

---

## 📌 Scope of Analysis

### 🛵 **Food Delivery**
- Onboarding and home screen experience
- Restaurant search, filters, and sorting
- Menu browsing, item details, cart, checkout
- Live tracking and delivery partner communication
- Loyalty programs (Zomato Gold/Pro)

### 🍴 **Dining Out**
- Restaurant discovery and table booking
- Dine-in offers and events
- User-generated content (photos, reviews)
- Loyalty integration and social proof

---

## 🔍 Frameworks Used

- **AARRR** (Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue)
- **HEART** (Happiness, Engagement, Adoption, Retention, Task Success)
- **SWOT Analysis**
- **User Journey Mapping**
- **Competitive Benchmarking** (e.g., Swiggy, Dineout)

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Highly personalized home and search interface
- Seamless food discovery and decision-making flow
- Strong social proof through reviews, photos, and ratings
- Clear focus on speed, reliability, and trust (e.g., hygiene badges)
- Dining vertical supports Zomato’s position as a holistic food platform

---

## 🧰 Tools Used

- Figma – UX flow diagrams
- Miro – Journey maps and personas
- Canva – SWOT diagrams and visual storytelling
- Excel/Sheets – Feature benchmarking
- (Optional) Python – Data analysis (if applicable)
